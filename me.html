Profile

I'm Rachelle,
Graphic Web designer

A communication manager graduate who majored in public relations, currently studying to become a web & graphic designer. While being a fulltime student, I freelance for clients. I create graphic designs and these designs vary from brochures, logo's, business cards, etc. Currently studying for Italian, which will be my fourth fluent language. My passions: food, travelling, writing & singing.

I prefer group activities and get energised by social interaction. I also tend to be more enthusiastic and easily excited.

Whenever I get the chance to go out of the country you'll find me travelling in de EU but also outside of it.

I want to improve in self-awareness and development. Because it’s the most powerful aspect in your life. It weighs very heavy on my heart. I do this by reading lots of self-development books, learning through apps (like Masterclass for example) and create extra curriculum by my own education.

The best way to get to know yourself is by travelling.

I tend to be very imaginative, open-minded and curious.

I love a challenge, so by pushhing myself out of my comfort-zone I can accelerate & improve my skills faster for myself, my colleagues and my superiors.

A few things I did to push myself out of my comfort-zone were; Shark Cage Diving, Bungee jumping of a bridge, Skydiving, Extreme Cave adventures and all this is South Africa. I opt for international internships and exchange programs wherever.

Comfort-zones are self-destructive

I’m sensitive and emotionally expressive. Therefore I’m a true empath and less competitive, though this depends on the situation. I can be very competitive when it comes to having to prove myself, or proving others wrong. I focus on harmony and cooperation.

I’m very reliable when it comes to tasks and projects. The last thing I want to do, is let someone down. I’m not afraid to capture an audience, and pick up on a mood and motivate in ways that allows me to communicate with emotion, passion, reason and restraint. I’ve been raised with values and norms to have respect, but also to get respect and be respected.

‘’Treat others like you want to be treated’’

I am decisive, thorough and highly organized. I value clarity, predictability (in a workflow) and closure, therefore in a work environment I prefer structure and planning because I can plan my other passions around it. Besides a stable work environment, I prefer spontaneity.

I once blamed my excitement & engagement with a lot of things all at once on my ADHD (which I used to have as a child), but figured out I'm a perfect example of a Scanner.

Scanners love to read and write, to fix and invent things, to design projects and businesses, to cook and sing, and to create the perfect dinner party. (You’ll notice I didn’t use the word “or,” because Scanners don’t love to do one thing or the other; they love them all.)

‘’To Scanners the world is like a big candy store full of fascinating opportunities, and all they want is to reach out and stuff their pockets.’’

I tend to be self-conscious and sensitive to stress. During projects I am likely to experience a wide range of emotions to be success-driven, perfectionistic and eager to improve.

I can be over idealistic and too selfless. This can result In me trying to please everyone and trying to get on their good side, which is self-destructive.

Because I’m very sensitive towards criticism, I have a fluctuating self-esteem. This depends on projects. Sometimes I feel like I’m not doing enough (because I’m perfectionistic), which can results in a change of mood and also a change in confidence which I normally exude.

Although I might scream ‘perfect candidate’ I also have my flaws.

But these flaws are there to help me grow on personal & professional level.
